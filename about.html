Our Story

Founded in 2014, Prodigy Infotech began as a small team of passionate developers with a vision to transform businesses through technology. Today, we're a leading digital solutions provider serving clients across the globe.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, client-centric approaches, and a commitment to excellence that has earned us the trust of over 500 satisfied clients.
